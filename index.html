About us

Industrial Revolution Business

At AA All Services Electrical, our goal is simple – to provide quality residential electrical services. We specialize in complete residential home wiring, service upgrades, and electrical inspections for home and business owners. We also work on wiring of small and large remodeling jobs including kitchens, bedrooms and additions to homes. We employ technicians who care, and each one is background-checked and screened for your peace of mind. Our team provides fast response times, and your satisfaction is guaranteed in all that we do. Whether you’re dealing with a malfunctioning circuit breaker or you need home air conditioner repair, our licensed electricians are available 24 hours a day for emergencies.
